The Founder

Meet Sanjana, the heart behind every petal.

What began as a quiet love for flowers in 2019 became a studio where memories are preserved into heirlooms. Sanjana started Creative Homes from her dining table — bridal bouquets pressed between cookbooks, resin curing on the kitchen counter. Today, the studio creates over 800 pieces a year, each one still made by her hands.

"I don't see resin as a craft. I see it as a quiet promise — that the moment you trusted me with, will still feel real ten, twenty, fifty years from now."
