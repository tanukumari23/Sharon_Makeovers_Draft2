The Founder

Meet Rebecca Megha Gomes, the artist behind every bridal glow.

Hi, I'm Rebecca Megha Gomes.

Before you're looking for a makeup artist, you're probably looking for peace of mind.

Someone who understands that you don't want to look like a different person on your wedding day. You want to look like you—just a little more radiant, confident, and camera-ready.

Over the last 8+ years, I've had the privilege of dolling up 350+ brides, each with her own skin, features, personality, and vision.

That's why my approach has never been about changing faces. It's about enhancing what already makes you beautiful, so when you look in the mirror, you still recognise yourself—only happier.

“Makeup should never change who you are. It should simply make you feel like the most confident and beautiful version of yourself.”

Mission

To make every bride feel confident & beautiful.

We believe bridal makeup should enhance natural beauty, not hide it. Every bride deserves a look that feels elegant, timeless and truly personal — allowing her confidence and happiness to shine through on her special day.

Vision

Becoming Delhi’s trusted soft glam bridal artist.

Our vision is to create a bridal experience that feels warm, comfortable and unforgettable. From intimate weddings to grand celebrations, Sharon Makeovers aims to help brides across India feel radiant, confident and celebrated in every moment.
